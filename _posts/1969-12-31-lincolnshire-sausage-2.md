<b>1240 Cal (8x 155)</b>
  
Ingredients:  
<ul>
	<li>2 Tbsp. bread crumbs</li>
	<li>1 tsp. salt</li>
	<li>1 tsp. pepper</li>
	<li>1 tsp. sage</li>
</ul>
Directions:  
<ol>
	<li>Combine 1 lb. ground pork with spices and mix well.</li>
	<li>Divide into 8 patties and fry over medium heat, approx. 5 minutes per side, until internal temp. is 160F.</li>
</ol>
