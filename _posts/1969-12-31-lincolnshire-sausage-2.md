1240 Cal (8x 155)
  
1/4 cup bread crumbs  
2 tsp. sage  
1 tsp. salt  
1 tsp. white pepper  
1 lb. ground pork

 * Combine all ingredients and mix well.
 * Divide into 8 patties and fry over medium heat, approx. 5 minutes per side, until internal temp. is 160F.
