<b>1240 Cal (8x 155)</b>
  
Ingredients:  

 * 2 Tbsp. bread crumbs
 * 1 tsp. salt
 * 1 tsp. pepper
 * 1 tsp. sage

Directions:  

 * Combine 1 lb. ground pork with spices and mix well.
 * Divide into 8 patties and fry over medium heat, approx. 5 minutes per side, until internal temp. is 160F.
