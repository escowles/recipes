**base:**

3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour  
2/3 cup water  
* weigh flour or measure by gently spooning into measuring cup and leveling off any
  excess

**tangzhong technique:**

3 tbsp. flour from above (30g)  
1/2 cup water from above  
* cook until thickened, about 1 1/2 - 2 1/2 minutes
* cool to lukewarm

1/2 cup milk  
4 tbsp. melted butter  
2 tbsp. sugar  
1 1/4 tsp. (8g) table salt  
2 tsp. instant yeast  
* add all ingredients to stand mixer and knead on medium-low for 5-7 minutes, until
  just cleaning the sides of the bowl, adding 1 tbsp. flour at a time as needed
  (sticking at bottom is ok)
* remove to a lightly greased bowl, cover, and rise for 1-2 hours
* punch down, shape into an 8" log and place in a greased 8 1/2" x 4 1/2" loaf pan
  and lightly cover with greased plastic wrap
* rise for 60 minutes, until it's about 1" over the top of the pan
* bake at 350°F for 30-35 minutes, until lightly browned and internal temperature
  reaches 190°F
* remove from pan and cool on a wire rack before slicing
