3 oz. tequila  
3 oz. cointreau  
4 oz. lime juice (3 1/2 limes)  
6 oz. invert syrup  
8 oz. water  
* combine ingredients and freeze in a plastic bag or ice cream maker

source: Sloshies, p. 44
