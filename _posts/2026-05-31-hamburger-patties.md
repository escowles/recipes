1 lb. ground beef  
1/2 tsp. baking soda  
1/2 tsp. salt  
1/4 tsp. garlic powder  
1/4 tsp. pepper
* mix all ingredients and divide into four balls
* shape into patties and rest 15 minutes before cooking
