2 Tbsp. paprika
1 Tbsp. black pepper
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper

* Mix all ingredients and store in airtight container.
* Rub on both sides of ribs and let sit.

**Mop Sauce:**
2 cups white vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp. salt

* Mix all ingredients and store in airtight container.
* Rub on both sides of ribs and let sit.
