1/2 cup soy sauce  
1/4 cup mirin  
1/4 cup rice vinegar (not sushi vinegar)  
1/4 cup sugar  
1/2 tsp. ground ginger  
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes  

* mix all ingredients and simmer for 2-3 minutes
