250 Cal (4x 63)

### Sauce:

1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. minced garlic
2 Tbsp. cooking sherry
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup soy sauce

* Mix all ingredients.

### Memphis Dry Rub Rub:

2 Tbsp. paprika  
1 Tbsp. black pepper  
1 Tbsp. brown sugar  
1 1/2 tsp. salt  
1 tsp. garlic salt  
1 tsp. cumin  
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper  

* Mix all ingredients and store in airtight container.
* Rub on both sides of ribs and let sit.

### Mop Sauce:
2 cups white vinegar  
1/2 cup water  
1 tsp. salt  

* Mix all ingredients and store in airtight container.
* Rub on both sides of ribs and let sit.
