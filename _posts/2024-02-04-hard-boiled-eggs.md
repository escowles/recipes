eggs  
water  

* gas stove:
  * place eggs in a medium pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil
  * cover pot, remove from heat, and cook for 12 minutes
  * remove to an ice bath for 15 minutes, then refrigerate until ready to use
* induction stove:
  * place eggs in a medium pot, cover with water, and cook on medium until boiling
  * boil for 2 minutes, then remove from heat, cover, and rest 15 minutes
  * remove to an ice bath for 15 minutes, then refrigerte until ready to use
