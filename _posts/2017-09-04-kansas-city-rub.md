1/3 cup brown sugar  
1/3 cup white sugar  
1 Tbsp. salt  
1 Tbsp. smoked paprkia  
1 Tbsp. onion salt  
1 Tbsp. celery salt  
1/2 Tbsp. black pepper  
1/2 Tbsp. chili powder  
1/2 tsp. mustard powder  
1/4 tsp. sage  
1/4 tsp. ginger  
1/4 tsp. garlic  
1/4 tsp. cumin  
1/8 tsp. coriander  
1/8 tsp. allspice  
1/8 tsp. chipotle pepper  

  * Mix all ingredients and shake over brisket, ribs, etc. before smoking.
