2 lbs. Montmorency cherries  
2 cups sugar  
3 cups water  
<ul>
 	<li>Bring to boil, then reduce heat.</li>
 	<li>Mash cherries, removing to a bowl in batches.</li>
 	<li>Simmer for 20 minutes.</li>
 	<li>Strain and refrigerate.</li>
</ul>
