* 12 lb. belgian pilsner malt
* 1 lb. belgian caramunich
* 1/2 lb. belgian biscuit

**15 minute kettle additions:**
* 1 tsp. Irish Moss

**yeast:**
* WLP530 Abbey Ale Yeast

**stats:**
* OG: 1.070
* FG: 1.016
* ABV: 7.1%
