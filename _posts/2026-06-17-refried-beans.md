1-2 slices bacon, chopped  
1/2 onion, chopped  
15 oz. can black or pinto beans, with liquid  
1/2 chipotle adobo, chopped, plus 1 tbsp. adobo sauce  
kosher salt  
* place bacon in a cold pan and heat slowly to render out fat
* add onion and sauté until softened
* add beans and chipotle, bring to a boil, and simmer for 5 minutes
* mash beans, remove from heat, add salt if needed
