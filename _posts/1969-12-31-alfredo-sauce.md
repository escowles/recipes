<b>1840 Cal (4x 480)</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p>
* 1/4 cup butter
* 1 cup heavy cream
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1 1/2 cups parmesan cheese, grated
* 1/4 cup parsley, chopped

<p>Directions:</p>

* Melt butter.  Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
* Add garlic and cheese, heat through.
* Add parsley and serve.
