2 tbsp. gochujang  
3 tbsp. brown sugar  
1 tbsp. soy sauce  
1 tsp. red pepper flakes  
3 cloves garlic, minced  
1" ginger, minced or grated  
1/2 tsp. black pepper  
1 lb. chicken, cubed  
* combine all ingredients and stir to combine
* chill for 2 hours

2 tbsp. neutral oil  
1/4 cup water  
marinated chicken  
6 oz. low-moisture mozzarella slices (optional)  
* heat oil and water in an oven-safe skillet or dutch oven
* stir fry chicken until chicken is cooked
* if using, top chicken with mozzarella and broil until melted and browned
* service with rice and roasted vegetables
