double jigger (1.5 oz.) gin  
juice from one lime  
tonic to taste  
* fill glass with ice
* pour gin and juice over ice and stir
* top with tonic to taste

---

4/25/23: we typically make these with a single jigger of simple syrup, properly a
"gin gimlet and tonic".
