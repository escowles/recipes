**oreo crust:**

24 Oreos  
4 Tbsp. butter, melted  
* process Oreos in food processor
* add butter and pulse to combine
* press into pie crust
* bake at 350°F for 5 minutes, then cool to room temperature


**peanut butter filling:**

1/2 cup real peanut butter  
4 oz. cream cheese, softened  
1/2 cup sugar  
1/2 tsp. vanilla  
1-2 tbsp. cream  
* stir peanut butter and cream cheese until combined
* stir in sugar and vanilla and continue mixing until well combined
* stir in cream, one tbsp. at a time until mixture reaches a spreadable texture
* spoon into crust, smooth out to level, and chill


**chocolate filling:**

1/2 cup cream  
5 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips  
1/2 cup cream  
* heat 1/2 cup cream until simmering then pour over chocolate chips
* rest for 2 minutes, then stir until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth
* rest 10 minutes, then beat remaining cream and fold into chocolate mixture
* pour into pie and chill
