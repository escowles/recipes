1000 Cal (4x 250)

1 cup fresh basil leaves  
6 Tbsp. olive oil  
6 Tbsp. parmesean cheese, grated  
2 cloves garlic  
2 Tbsp. pine nuts  

* Blend basil, olive oil and parmesean in food processor.
* Add pine nutes and chopped garlic.
* Blend until smooth.
