640 Cal (4x 160)

1 1/2 lbs. potatoes  
1 1/3 Tbsp. olive oil  
1 Tbsp. dried rosemary  
salt & pepper  

* Cube potatoes and toss with oil and spices.
* Bake at 450F for 30 minutes, turning halfway through.
