<b>640 Cal (4x 160)</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p><ul>
<li>1 1/2 lbs.	potatoes</li>
<li>1 1/3 Tbsp.	olive oil</li>
<li>1 Tbsp.	dried rosemary</li>
<li>	salt & pepper</li>
</ul>
<p>Directions:</p>
<ol>
<li>Cube potatoes and toss with oil and spices.</li>
<li>Bake at 450F for 30 minutes, turning halfway through.</li>
</ol>
