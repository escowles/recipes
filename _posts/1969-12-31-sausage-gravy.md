<b>1900 Cal (6x 315)</b>
  
Ingredients:  

 * 1 lb. sausage
 * 1/4 cup flour
 * 2 cups milk
 * 1/2 tsp. salt
 * 1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:  

 * Brown sausage until finely crumbled.
 * Add flour and mix well, then add milk and spices and cook until thickened.
