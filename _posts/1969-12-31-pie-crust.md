<b>1170 Cal (8x 146)</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p><ul>
<li>1 cup	flour</li>
<li>1/2 tsp.	salt</li>
<li>6 1/3 Tbsp.	shortening</li>
<li>3 Tbsp.	water</li>
</ul>
<p>Directions:</p>
<ol>
<li>Mix flour and salt in large mixing bowl.</li>
<li>Mix in shortening with pastry blender until it's broken down into pea-sized chunks.</li>
<li>Add water and mix thoroughly.</li>
</ol>
