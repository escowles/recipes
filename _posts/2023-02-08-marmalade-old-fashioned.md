1 tsp. orange marmalade  
3-4 dashes Angostura bitters  
60ml rye whiskey  
slice orange peel  
* mix marmalade, bitters, and 20ml of the whiskey in a tumbler
* add ice and remaining whiskey, and stir
* garnish with orange peel
