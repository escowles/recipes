5 cups blueberries  
2 Tbsp. sugar  
3 Tbsp. flour  
1 Tbsp. lemon juice, plus zest  
* toss and add to 2 quart baking dish (9" x 9")

1/4 cup butter, softened  
1/2 cup brown sugar  
1/4 cup flour  
3/4 cup oats  
1/4 tsp. cinnamon  
* combine all ingredients and break up any lumps
* sprinkle over blueberries
* bake at 375Â°F for 35-40 minutes, until top is browned and filling is bubbling
