1 cup heavy cream  
1 cup whole milk  
1 can sweetened condensed milk  
1/2 cup lime juice (from 4-5 limes)  
zest from 1 lime  
1/8 tsp. salt  
1/3 cup graham cracker chunks (optional)  
* combine all ingredients except graham crackers into a bowl and mix well (the
  mixture will thicken as the acid and milk react)  
* cover and chill for at least 4 hours  
* process in ice cream maker, adding graham cracker chunks at the end (if using)
* freze to firm up
