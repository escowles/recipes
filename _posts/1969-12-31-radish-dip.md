<b>800 Cal (8x 100)</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p><ul>
<li>2 cloves	garlic</li>
<li>3	radishes</li>
<li>8 oz.	cream cheese</li>
</ul>
<p>Directions:</p>
<ol>
<li>Mince garlic and radishes in food processor.</li>
<li>Add cream cheese and mix until blended.</li>
<li>Chill until ready to serve.</li>
</ol>
