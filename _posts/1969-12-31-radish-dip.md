<b>800 Cal (8x 100)</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p>
* 2 cloves garlic
* 3 radishes
* 8 oz. cream cheese

<p>Directions:</p>

* Mince garlic and radishes in food processor.
* Add cream cheese and mix until blended.
* Chill until ready to serve.
