2 slices cucumber  
6 mint leaves  
3/4 lime  
4 oz. Pimm's #1  
1 oz. gin  
ice  
5 oz. ginger ale  
fruit garnishes  

  * Muddle the cucumber, 4 mint leaves, and 1/4 lime
  * Shake with ice, Pimm's, and gin
  * Strain into ice-filled tumbler
  * Fill out with ginger ale
  * Garnish with last 2 mint leaves, plus any fruit garnishes
