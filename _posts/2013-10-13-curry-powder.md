1½ tsp. cumin  
1½ tsp. coriander  
1½ tsp. turmeric  
¼ tsp. red pepper flakes  
¼ tsp. ginger  
¼ tsp. garlic powder  
¼ tsp. pepper  
  
Combine in food processor and pulse until powdered and blended.
