2 cups. bread crumbs  
4 tsp. salt    
2 tsp. sage  
1 tsp. parsley  
1 tsp. thyme  
1 tsp. white pepper  
3/4 tsp. coriander  
3/4 tsp. nutmeg  
1/2 tsp. black pepper  
1/2 tsp. mace  
1/2 tsp. brown sugar  
1/8 tsp. cayenne or chipotle pepper  
2.5 lb. ground pork shoulder  

* mix bread crumbs and spices
* mix into pork and form patties
