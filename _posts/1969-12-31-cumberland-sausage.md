1240 Cal (8x 155)
  
2 Tbsp. bread crumbs  
1 tsp. nutmeg  
1/2 tsp. mace  
1/2 tsp. salt  
1/2 tsp. pepper  
1 lb. ground pork

* mix bread crumbs and spices
* mix into pork and form patties
