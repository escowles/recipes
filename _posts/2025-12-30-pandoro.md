**biga:**

1/2 cup flour  
1 1/2 tsp. yeast  
1 tsp. sugar  
3 tbsp. warm water  
* mix in a small bowl and set aside overnight

**dough:**

biga  
450g flour  
125g sugar  
1/2 tsp. salt  
1 1/2 tsp. yeast  
zest of 1 lemon  
zest of 1 orange (or two tangerines)  
150g butter, softened  
100ml warm milk  
2 eggs  
2 yolks  
2 tsp. vanilla  
* add all ingredients to stand mixer and mix on low speed until a sticky dough forms
* increase speed to medium and mix for 10 minutes, until the dough sticks to the
  hook when raised (mix for another 5 minutes if it drops)
* turn out onto a floured board and knead, then add to a lightly greased bowl, cover,
  and rest until doubled (which can take 4 hours or longer)
* turn out onto a floured board and knead to knock out the air, then add to a greased
  tin. cover and rest until it rises above the top of the tin (1 1/2 - 2 hours)
* bake at 350Â°F for 35-40 minutes, testing with a skewer that it is cooked through
* cool in the tin for 5 minutes before turning out onto a cooling rack to cool
  completely
