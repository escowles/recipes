1 egg  
1/4 tsp. vanilla  
1 cup almond milk  
1/4 cup vegetable oil  
1 1/2 tsp. sugar  
2 tsp. baking powder  
1/4 tsp. salt  
1 cup flour  

  * Beat egg, then add liquids and beat again.
  * Add sugar, baking powder, and salt and beat again.
  * Add flour and mix until just combined.
  * Prepare according to waffle maker instructions.
