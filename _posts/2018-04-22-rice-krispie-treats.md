1/2 cup peanut butter  
1/2 cup maple syrup  
2 cups rice krispies  

  * Melt peanut butter and maple syrup in a sauce pan
  * Combine with rice krispies and press into greased/lined cookie sheet
  * Chill for 30 minutes to firm up
