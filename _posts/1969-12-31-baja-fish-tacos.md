3400 Cal (12x 283)

### Sauce:
1/2 cup mayonnaise  
1 Tbsp. lime juice  
1 tsp. cilantro, chopped  
1 dried chipotle, soaked in boiling water  
1/4 tsp. salt  

* Combine all ingredients and mix until smooth.

### Beer-Battered Cod Fillets (or substitute frozen):

12 cod fillets  
1 1/2 cup flour  
3 eggs  
8 oz. dark beer  
2 tsp. dry mustard  
1 Tbsp. chili powder  
1 Tbsp. salt  
1 tsp. sugar  

* Dredge cod fillets in flour
* Coat with batter and fry in oil until browned.

### Assembly:

12 corn tortillas  
2 cups cabbage, chopped  

* Spread sauce on tortilla and add fried fillet.
* Top with cabbage.
