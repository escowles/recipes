22 Cal

1 lemon
1 Tbsp. kosher salt

* Cut lemon into sixths lengthwise, sprinkle with salt and place in non-metal dish.
* Cover with water and cook at 250F for 3 hours.
* Let cool and refrigerate, up to 6 months covered in glass jar.
