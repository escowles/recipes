2 parts paprika  
2 parts crushed peppercorns  
2 parts kosher salt  
2 parts garlic powder  
1 part onion powder  
1 part coriander  
1 part dill weed  
1 part red pepper flakes  
* mix together and use as a steak seasoning, marinade spice, etc.
* scale up to meet your needs: using 1/4 tsp. as the unit yields 1 Tbsp.
