**grain bill:**
* 7 1/2 lbs. pilsner DME
* 12 oz. dark candi syrup (275L)
* 8 oz. caramunich II
* 5 oz. biscuit
* 2 1/2 oz. chocolate

**45 minute kettle additions:**
* 2 oz. Styrian Goldings hop pellets

**15 minute kettle additions:**
* 1 tsp. Irish Moss

**yeast:**
* WLP530 Abbey Ale Yeast

**stats:**
* OG: 1.082
* FG: 1.014
* ABV: 8.2%

* first batch in new jersey
