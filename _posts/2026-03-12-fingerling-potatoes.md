1 1/2 lbs. fingerling potatoes  
2 tbsp. olive oil  
1 tsp. salt  
pepper, rosemary, thyme to taste  
* wash potatoes and slice lengthwise
* toss with oil and spices
* heat baking sheet at 400°F and place potatoes cut side down
* bake 15 minutes, then stir and bake an additional 15 minutes until crisp
