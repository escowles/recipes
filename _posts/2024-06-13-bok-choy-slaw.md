2 tbsp. rice vinegar  
2 tsp. sesame oil  
1 tsp. sugar  
1 tsp. dijon mustard  
1/4 tsp. salt  
* whisk in a large bowl

8 oz. baby bok choy (about 8 heads), thinly sliced  
2 shallots, halved and thinly sliced  
* add to sauce and toss to coat
* chill for 1 hour

source: <https://www.eatingwell.com/recipe/249249/crunchy-bok-choy-slaw/>
