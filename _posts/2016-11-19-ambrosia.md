1 cup shredded coconut  
1 cup mandarin orange segments, drained  
1 cup pineapple chunks, drained  
1 cup sour cream  
1 cup miniature marshmallows  
<ul>
 	<li>Combine all ingredients and mix well.</li>
 	<li>Chill 30 minutes before serving.</li>
</ul>
