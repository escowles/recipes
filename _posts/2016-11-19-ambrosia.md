1 cup shredded coconut  
1 cup mandarin orange segments, drained  
1 cup pineapple chunks, drained  
1 cup sour cream  
1 cup miniature marshmallows  

  * Combine all ingredients and mix well.
  * Chill 30 minutes before serving.
