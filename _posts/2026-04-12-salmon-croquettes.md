1 tbsp. olive oil  
1/4 onion, diced  
1/2 green bell pepper, finely chopped 
1 clove garlic, minced  
* sauté onion and bell pepper until softened, 3-5 minutes
* add garlic and sauté another 30 seconds

14 oz. can salmon  
1 egg, lightly beaten  
1/4 cup flour  
1 tbsp. parsley (dry) 
1 tsp. old bay  
1 tsp. hot sauce  
½ tsp. kosher salt   
½ tsp. black pepper  
* add to veggies and mix well
* divide into 4 portions, shape into thick patties, and chill 30 minutes

1/4 cup flour  
1 egg, beaten  
1/2 cup bread crumbs  
2 tsp. onion powder, garlic powder, paprika or a combination (1 tsp. garlic powder,
1/2 tsp. old bay, 1/4 tsp. paprika, 1/4 tsp. chipotle)  
1/4 cup canola oil  
* mix bread crumbs and seasoning in one bowl, then flour and egg in two other bowls
* dip each patty in flour, then egg, then bread crumbs
* fry in oil for 3-5 minutes per side, until well-browned

source: <https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1023040-salmon-croquettes>

took me so long to bread them that the first one burned before i got the last one

next time: squeeze liquid out of salmon, increase binder, and maybe partially-freeze
