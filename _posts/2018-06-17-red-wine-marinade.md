1 cup red wine  
3/4 cup olive oil  
1 Tbsp. lemon juice  
1 tsp. salt  
1 tsp. pepper  
5 cloves of garlic, minced  
1/4 onion, diced  
1 Tbsp. rosemary  

  * Combine all ingredients with steaks, turning to coat well
  * Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, turning several times
