1/4 cup warm milk  
2 tbsp. warm water  
1 1/4 tsp. yeast  
1/2 tsp. brown sugar  
* mix yeast and sugar into milk/water and rest 10-15 minutes until bubbly

1 cup + 2 tbsp. flour  
2 tsp. oil  
1/2 tsp. salt  
* stir into yeast mixture until a dough ball forms
* transfer to a lightly-floured surface and knead until smooth, about 3 minutes
* transfer to a greased bowl, cover and rise until doubled, about 1 hour
* divide into 4 portions and roll each into a ball and rest 5 minutes
* roll each ball into a 7" disk
* heat a lightly-oiled skillet and fry each disk until lightly browned and puffed,
  about 30 seconds per side
* brush with butter or garlic butter if desired and serve warm
