juice and zest from 1 large navel orange, 2 cara caras, or 4 clementines  
1/3 cup (75g) sugar  
3 egg yolks  
1 1/2 tbsp. (21g) cold butter, cubed  
* process zest and sugar in a food processor to make sure the zest is very fine
* combine with juice and yolks in a saucepan over low heat while constantly stirring,
  until bubbling and reaching 175Â°F
* remove from heat and add butter one cube at a time, whisking to incorporate
* pour into a jar and let cool before refrigerating

source: <https://lovefoodies.com/how-to-make-orange-or-clementine-curd/>

11/04/2025: made this last night with 2 cara caras (which produced about 50ml juice and
20g zest). it was mild, but rich and flavorful. i can see why some recipes add lemon
juice to sharpen the curd, but i think it's fine for it to be milder and sweeter.
