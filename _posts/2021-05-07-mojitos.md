10 mint leaves  
1/2 lime cut into wedges  
2 Tbsp. sugar  
1 cup ice cubes  
double jigger white rum  
1/2 cup club soda  
* muddle mint leaves and one lime wedge
* muddle remaining lime wedgees and sugar
* add remaining ingredients and stir
