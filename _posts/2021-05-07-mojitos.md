1/2 lime, juice and peel  
10 mint leaves  
single jigger (.75 oz.) simple syrup  
double jigger (1.5 oz.) white rum  
* shake over ice and strain into ice-filled glass
* top up with club soda, garnish with mint sprig and lime slice
