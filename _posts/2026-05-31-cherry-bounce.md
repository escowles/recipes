14.5 oz. can tart cherries  
1/2 cup sugar  
2 cups brandy, bourbon, or other liquor  
1/2 cinnamon stick  
2 cloves  
pinch nutmeg  
pinch allspice  
* add to 1-quart jar and shake until sugar is dissolved
* store in a cool, dark spot for 6 weeks, shaking occasionally
* strain to remove solids and drink neat or with club soda or sprite
