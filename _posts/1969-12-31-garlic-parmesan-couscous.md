500 Cal (4x 125)

1 cup water  
3/4 cup couscous  
2 Tbsp. butter  
3 cloves garlic  
4 Tbsp. Parmesan cheese  
2 green onions  

* Bring water to boil.  Stir in couscous, cover and remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.
* Melt butter and saute garlic.  Add to couscous with remaining ingredients and fluff.
