1 egg  
1/4 cup flour  
? milk  

* mix egg and flour until smooth, adding milk slowly
* 3 oz. makes 10-12" pancake, tilt pan to spread out
* hot pan greased with butter, oil, or lard
  
Source: <http://zimeon.com/1997/02/pancakes>
