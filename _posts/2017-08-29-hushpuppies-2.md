2 cups cornmeal  
½ cup flour  
2 Tbsp.  sugar  
2 tsp. baking powder  
1 tsp. salt  
½ tsp. baking soda  
1 egg, beaten  
¾ cup milk  
¾ cup cream-style corn  
oil for frying  

* Mix dry ingredients, then add egg, milk and corn, stirring until just mixed.
* Drop spoonfuls of batter into 375° oil and fry until golden brown.
* Dry on paper towels and eat warm.
