2,080 Cal (8x 260)  
  
1 1/2 cups flour  
1/3 cup cocoa  
1 tsp. baking soda  
1 cup sugar  
1/2 tsp. salt  
5 Tbsp. vegetable oil  
1 cup water  
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla  
1 Tbsp. cider or white vinegar  

 * Mix dry ingredients.
 * In a separate bowl, whisk wet ingredients.
 * Combine and whisk until smooth.
 * Tap mixing bowl or drop from 6 inches to shake out air bubbles.
 * Bake at 350Â°F for 25-30 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean.
 * Cool before removing from pan.
 * Dust with powdered sugar or frost as desired.
