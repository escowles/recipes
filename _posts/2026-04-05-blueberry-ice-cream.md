12 oz. fresh blueberries  
1/2 cup sugar  
juice from 1/2 lime  
1 tbsp. water  
* heat until berries burst, then purée until smooth

1 cup milk  
1 cup cream  
1 tsp. vanilla  
1/8 tsp. salt  
* add to blueberry mixture, mix well and chill at least 4 hours
* process in ice cream maker, then freeze to firm up
