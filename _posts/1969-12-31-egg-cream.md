<b>210 Cal</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p><ul>
<li>2 Tbsp.	chocolate syrup (preferably Kosher-for-Passover U-Bet)</li>
<li>6 oz.	whole milk</li>
<li>6 oz.	soda water</li>
</ul>
<p>Directions:</p>
<ol>
<li>Mix milk and chocolate syrup in glass.</li>
<li>Add soda water.</li>
</ol>
