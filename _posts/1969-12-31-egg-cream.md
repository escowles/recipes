<b>210 Cal</b>
<p>Ingredients:</p>
* 2 Tbsp. chocolate syrup (preferably Kosher-for-Passover U-Bet)
* 6 oz. whole milk
* 6 oz. soda water

<p>Directions:</p>

* Mix milk and chocolate syrup in glass.
* Add soda water.
