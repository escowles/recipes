1/2 lb. beef  
1/2 onion, chopped  
* press beef in even layer on the bottom of a skillet and sprinkle with onion
* cook undisturbed until meat is well-browned, 6-8 minutes

3/4 tsp. cumin  
1/4 tsp. chipotle powder  
1/4 tsp. oregano  
salt and pepper, to taste  
* add spices to beef mixture and stir while breaking the beef up into small pieces
* continue cooking until onion is softened, 2-4 minutes

3/4 cup black or pinto beans  
1/2 tomato, chopped  
* add to beef mixture and continue cooking until liquids are boiled off

1 tsp. lime juice  
4 oz. shredded cheese (jack or mexican blend)  
* stir into beef mixture and remove from heat
* fill burrito-sized tortillas and roll to seal, sear in a hot pan if desired
