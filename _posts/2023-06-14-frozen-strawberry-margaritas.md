2.5 oz. tequila  
2.5 oz. cointreau  
2.5 oz. lime juice (2 limes)  
3 oz. simple syrup  
6 oz. ice  
6 oz. frozen strawberries  
* combine all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth
* or substitute ice and pureed fresh strawberries and freeze in an ice cream maker
