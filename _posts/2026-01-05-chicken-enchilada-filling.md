1 tbsp. butter  
2 tbsp. flour  
1/2 cup chicken broth  
* make a roux then slowly whisk in broth to make a smooth sauce

3/4 - 1 lb. shredded chicken  
1/4 tsp. onion powder  
1/4 tsp. garlic powder  
1/4 tsp. pepper  
1/4 tsp. cumin  
1/4 tsp. chipotle powder  
* add chicken and spices, then mix well

1/4 cup milk  
* stir in up to 1/4 cup milk to desired consistency

source: Copycat Restaurant Favorites, p. 141 and
        <https://thecozycook.com/cream-of-chicken-soup-recipe/>
