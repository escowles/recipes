2 lemons  
1/4 cup olive oil  
2 tbsp. rice or apple cider vinegar  
2 cloves garlic  
1 shallot  
1 cup fresh basil leaves  
1 cup spinach  
1/3 cup nutritional yeast  
1/4 cup almonds  
1 tsp. salt  
* add all ingredients to blender (liquids first)
* blend until smooth

source: <https://iamafoodblog.com/tiktok-green-goddess-salad/>
